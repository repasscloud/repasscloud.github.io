LunaVPN

Service Status

LunaVPN is no longer operational and is not an active service operated by RePass Cloud Pty Ltd. All hosted service accounts are closed, subscriptions are not renewed, and support is not provided.

This page is retained for historical reference only.

About LunaVPN

LunaVPN was a VPN service built to provide online privacy and security using modern VPN technology.

Technology

WireGuard protocol: LunaVPN was built on WireGuard, selected for its modern cryptography, efficient design, and minimal attack surface. WireGuard provides a fast, auditable protocol with strong encryption primitives and significantly less code surface than older VPN protocols.

Dedicated isolated instances: LunaVPN was designed around dedicated, isolated instances so customers did not share VPN resources with other users. This reduced noisy-neighbour risk and improved isolation compared to shared VPN infrastructure.

Global accessibility: LunaVPN offered secure internet access from multiple regions. Server availability and locations varied over time based on capacity and operational requirements.

Simplicity and transparency: LunaVPN aimed to provide a straightforward setup experience with clear product expectations, avoiding hidden fees and unclear configurations.

Privacy and Logging

LunaVPN was designed with privacy as a core principle. Operational logging was limited to what was necessary to run the service reliably and securely.

Why LunaVPN Existed

LunaVPN was built to reduce exposure to invasive tracking and protect traffic over untrusted networks, with a particular focus on travellers and remote users needing secure connectivity on public networks. A single instance could be used across multiple devices depending on configuration and plan limits at the time.

Open Source Availability

The LunaVPN source code has been made available as open source for community use. This does not mean the LunaVPN hosted service remains active or supported by RePass Cloud. Any use of the open source code is at your own risk and subject to the licence terms in the relevant repository.
