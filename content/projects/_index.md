## Active Projects

### Cinturon360

[Cinturon360](https://cinturon360.com) is our actively developed and maintained enterprise SaaS platform. It is the operational control layer for organisations that need to manage complexity across customers, partners, internal teams, and business units — under structured governance, with full visibility and control.

Cinturon360 is built for organisations operating in layered environments where different entities require different levels of access, configuration, and accountability. It provides a governed foundation for operational workflows, tenant-aware configuration, secure API integrations, identity and access management, and full audit logging.

Key capabilities include multi-tenant architecture with strong isolation, Microsoft Entra ID integration, role-based permissions, delegated administration, operational automation, and regional hosting flexibility to meet data residency requirements.

Cinturon360 is not a general-purpose workflow tool. It is a platform for running complex operations in a controlled, auditable, and scalable way.

## Custom Tooling & Automation

Beyond Cinturon360, RePass Cloud designs and builds a broad range of custom tools, automation systems, and operational software for enterprise and business customers. This includes cloud infrastructure tooling, Microsoft 365 and identity automation, finance workflow systems, FinOps platforms, PowerShell modules, and Windows environment utilities.

These engagements are not named products — they are purpose-built solutions delivered to specific organisations. A selection of real-world examples is documented in our [case studies](/case-studies/).

## Retired Projects

The following projects were built and operated by RePass Cloud and have since been retired. All have been released as open source and are available on GitHub for community use. RePass Cloud does not operate hosted services for any of these products and does not provide support for them.

1. [**LunaVPN**](/projects/lunavpn) — Privacy-focused VPN service built on the WireGuard protocol with dedicated isolated instances. *Retired. Open source.*

2. [**OptechX**](/projects/optechx) — SaaS platform for building custom Windows Standard Operating Environment images for enterprise deployment. *Retired. Open source.*

3. [**WanderConnect**](/projects/wanderconnect) — App concept for discovering local experiences and planning memorable outings. *Retired. Open source.*

4. [**TigerGrab 「とらグラブ」**](/projects/tigergrab) — Windows utility for downloading and preserving online videos for personal archiving. *Retired. Open source.*

5. [**CurseDelete**](/projects/cursedelete) — Cross-platform command-line utility for recursively deleting directories at high speed, across Windows, macOS, and Linux. *Retired. Open source.*

6. [**CveInfo**](/projects/cveinfo) — Command-line utility for querying CVE vulnerability information, designed for integration into security operations and patch management workflows. *Retired. Open source.*
