## Service Status

TigerGrab is **retired** and is no longer an active product or service operated by RePass Cloud Pty Ltd. The product is no longer actively maintained or supported by RePass Cloud.

This page is retained for historical reference only.

## About TigerGrab

TigerGrab 「とらグラブ」 was a Windows utility created for users who wanted a simple, reliable way to download and preserve online videos for personal archiving. It was designed to be straightforward to use without requiring technical configuration or third-party dependencies managed by the user.

## Open Source Availability

TigerGrab has been released as open source and is available on GitHub. RePass Cloud does not provide an operated hosted service for TigerGrab. Any use of the source code is at your own risk and subject to the licence terms in the repository.
