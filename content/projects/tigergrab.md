## Service status

TigerGrab is **retired** and is no longer an active product or service operated
by RePass Cloud Pty Ltd.

- No customers are live on RePass Cloud operated TigerGrab systems.
- The product is no longer actively maintained or supported by RePass Cloud.

## Open source availability

TigerGrab has been released as open source and is available on GitHub.

- RePass Cloud does not provide an operated hosted service for TigerGrab.
- Any use of the source code is at your own risk and subject to the license
  terms in the repository.

## About TigerGrab

TigerGrab 「とらグラブ」 was created for Windows users who wanted a simple way to
download and preserve online videos for personal archiving.

This page is retained for historical reference only.
