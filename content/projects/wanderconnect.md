## Service Status

WanderConnect is **retired** and is no longer an active product or service operated by RePass Cloud Pty Ltd. The product is no longer actively maintained or supported by RePass Cloud.

This page is retained for historical reference only.

## About WanderConnect

WanderConnect was a product concept focused on helping people discover local places and plan memorable experiences — including romantic dates, family outings, and solo exploration. It aimed to surface relevant local options in a way that reduced the friction of planning and discovery.

## Open Source Availability

WanderConnect has been released as open source and is available on GitHub. RePass Cloud does not operate a hosted service for WanderConnect. Any use of the source code is at your own risk and subject to the licence terms in the repository.
