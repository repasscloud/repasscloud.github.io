## Service status

WanderConnect is **retired** and is no longer an active product or service
operated by RePass Cloud Pty Ltd.

- No customers are live on RePass Cloud operated WanderConnect systems.
- The product is no longer actively maintained or supported by RePass Cloud.

## Open source availability

WanderConnect has been released as open source and is available on GitHub.

- RePass Cloud does not operate a hosted service for WanderConnect.
- Any use of the source code is at your own risk and subject to the license
  terms in the repository.

## About WanderConnect

WanderConnect was a concept and product direction focused on helping people
discover local places and plan memorable experiences, including romantic dates,
family outings, and solo exploration.

This page is retained for historical reference only.
