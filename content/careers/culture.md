## Who We Are

RePass&nbsp;Cloud is a small, technically focused team building cloud infrastructure, APIs, and software systems that matter. We're a mixed bunch - different backgrounds, different time zones, different stories. Everyone is welcome here.

We're not a corporate enterprise and we'll never try to be. We move fast, we take ownership, and we get things done. If you'd rather ship something great than attend a meeting about it, you'll fit right in.

## How We Work

We operate on a hybrid model with genuine flexibility. Some of our team works fully remotely; others work from an office that isn't near our Sydney head office. What we care about is output, communication, and reliability - not where you sit.

We are a digital-first organisation. Flexible working arrangements are the norm, not the exception.

## What We Value

### Autonomy

We trust our people to manage their own work. You won't be micromanaged. You're expected to take ownership of your domain, raise blockers early, and deliver on your commitments.

### Engineering Excellence

We care deeply about quality - not perfection at the cost of progress, but a genuine commitment to building things well, understanding trade-offs, and continuously improving.

### Small Team Ownership

In a small team, everyone's contribution matters. We don't have layers of people to absorb mistakes or ambiguity. Each person owns their piece and is accountable to the team.

### Innovation

We encourage experimentation, fresh thinking, and challenging assumptions. If you have a better way to do something, say so. We'd rather hear it than not.

## What We Expect

Our engineers and contractors are expected to:

- Work independently and manage their own priorities
- Solve complex, ambiguous problems without waiting to be told what to do
- Communicate clearly - with the team, with clients, and with stakeholders

We work with people, not just code. The human element matters.

## Diversity & Inclusion

We explicitly support diverse and inclusive hiring. Hiring decisions are based on three things:

- **Skills** - can you do the work?
- **Experience** - have you done it, or can you demonstrate you're capable?
- **Professional conduct** - do you engage with integrity, respect others, and represent the brand well?

Professional conduct doesn't mean suits and formality. We don't care what you wear. We care about how you show up.

## Tools We Use

We provide our team with access to the tools they need to do their best work - including AI. We give employees and contractors access to Claude by Anthropic, ChatGPT, Google Gemini, Perplexity, Grok, and whatever else supports the work.

We use AI to supplement and enhance our own capabilities, not to replace the thinking. The expectation is that AI is used in the best interest of the organisation and the client.
