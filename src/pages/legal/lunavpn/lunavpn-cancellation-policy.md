Last updated: 2026-02-22

## 1. Status of LunaVPN

LunaVPN is closed and is no longer an active service operated by RePass Cloud
Pty Ltd ("RePass Cloud", "we", "us", or "our").

- No new subscriptions are offered.
- No renewals are processed.
- No customer support is provided for LunaVPN.
- LunaVPN accounts are closed and access is terminated.

If you are looking for an actively maintained RePass Cloud product, refer to
our current offerings on our [main site](/).

## 2. Subscriptions, Billing, and Charges

LunaVPN subscriptions are not renewed and should no longer be billed by us.

If you believe you are still being charged for LunaVPN:

- Check your payment provider statement for the merchant name.
- Contact your bank or card provider to dispute unauthorised charges if needed.
- Email us at [contact page](/contact) so we can help identify the source of the charge where possible.

## 3. Account Closure and Service Termination

All LunaVPN accounts are closed and service access is terminated. Any remaining
licences, VPN entitlements, or server allocations associated with the former
service are decommissioned.

## 4. Data Handling

Where RePass Cloud controlled LunaVPN operational data, we have taken
reasonable steps to wind down and delete service data associated with closed
accounts, subject to any legal obligations that require limited retention
(for example accounting records).

If you have a specific privacy request relating to LunaVPN, contact:
[contact page](/contact)

## 5. Open Source Availability

The LunaVPN source code has been made available as open source for community
use. This does not mean the LunaVPN service remains active or supported by
RePass Cloud. Any use of the open source code is at your own risk.
